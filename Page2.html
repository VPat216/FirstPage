Cars Trilogy

Description

The Cars franchise is a series of animated films created by Pixar Animation Studios. .

The films follow the adventures of a group of anthropomorphic cars and trucks, with the first film, Cars, released in 2006, and the latest film, Cars 3, released in 2017
